Bobi Hatisi


Bio

Born in Cairo, of Romanian parents, raised in Germany, Robert Hatisi moved to France to study at the drama-school ESAD in Paris. Since then he has performed on stage Shakespeare, Racine, Marivaux, Dumas, Claudel, Schwab, Copi and meets Meriam Korichi as director and dramatist of Euripides and Claudel. He is a founding member of J.C.Meurisse’s company « Chiens de Navarre » and performs in France (Bouffes du Nord, Centre Pompidou, MAC de Créteil, Théâtre de Gennevilliers etc) and abroad ( Switzerland, Belgium, Canada, Bosnia and USA). Simultaneously, his aesthetic research leads him to create cinematographic objects based on collage, pop-up and animation as the short-movie « Babylonian Candour » (2015) and the children’s play « Un Roi sans Réponse », performed at Paris-Villette and The International Visual Theatre (2016). In June 2014 he takes part at the 4th edition of « A Night of Philosophy » in Berlin, starting a collaboration with Meriam Korichi of an open and on going project within these events.
